# US-051: City & Industry Landing Pages

**Epic:** Post-Launch Enhancements

## Description
- Use CMS templates to spin up localized pages (Pueblo, Canon City, Denver, Monument, etc.) and industry minisites (Medical, Legal, Defense).
- Each page requires localized copy, testimonials, schema.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Minimum 4 city pages and 3 industry pages live with unique content.
- Internal linking from main site navigation and sitemap.
- Performance & SEO checks pass.

## Dependencies
- CMS + design system.
