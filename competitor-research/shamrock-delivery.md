# Shamrock Delivery

- **Website:** https://shamrockdelivery.com/
- **Heritage:** 35+ years in business; family-owned.

## Services
- Same-day courier and **refrigerated delivery** throughout Colorado; specializes in on-demand, timeframes as fast as 45 minutes.
- Vehicle mix includes bikes, cars/vans, and refrigerated cargo vans. Focus on Denver metro, Fort Collins, Colorado Springs [Source](https://shamrockdelivery.com/).
- Offers online ordering, real-time GPS-integrated dispatch, bonded and uniformed drivers, and customized billing/proof of signature.

## Differentiators
- Refrigerated fleet and cold-chain messaging—strong for food, pharma, floral industries.
- Online ordering + technology narrative (“sophisticated software integrates real-time ordering, dispatch, GPS”).

## Implications for Rap Express
- Need to decide whether to enter refrigerated/cold-chain niche or partner with specialized carriers.
- Rap can borrow their technology narrative by showcasing Courier Complete integrations, online portal, and automated invoicing.
- Emphasize Springs-local identity vs Shamrock’s Denver-first brand.
